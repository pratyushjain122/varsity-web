Collaborations

Abhyuday, IIT Bombay

Two webinars have been conducted in collaboration with Abhyuday, the Social Club of IIT Bombay.

Girl's Up

A virtual event to celebrate the International Girl's Day was conducted in collaboration with Girl's Up Heron.

Rotaract Club of Thane North End

A food donation drive for the International Day of Charity was a combined effort of V@arSITycare and Rotaract Club of Thane North End.

Robin Hood Army

Robin Hood Academy’s students to Symbiosis Institute of Technology, in collaboration with V@rSITy-Care conducted a Swachata Pledge with kids

Karishma Care

A small initiative by V@rsity-care along with Karishmaa Care conducted a drive to help the victims of ‘Maharashtra Floods’

Paani Foundation

V@rSITy Care joined the Maharashramdaan, a rainwater harvesting and watershed management project arranged by the Paani Foundation, to make Maharashtra drought free

Vasundhra Swachhta Abhiyan

V@rSITy Care joined hands with an umbrella organization, Vashundhara Swachata Abhiyan, Pune with the mission of reforestation of the hills, afforestation- pure air ,water percolation, waste management and plastic-free lifestyle

Symbiosis Centre for Waste Resource Management

A guest lecture on “Waste Management in Symbiosis Campus” was held by the department of Civil Engineering (CESS) and V@rSITy-care to provide the students with an opportunity to learn about the techniques of water management in the campus.
