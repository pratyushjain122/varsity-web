WEBINAR 1 : DEALING WITH UNCERTAINTIES

DATE: 13 June, 2020 ORGANIZATION: ABHYUDAY , The Social body of IIT Bombay LOCATION: Virtual On 13th June 2020, a webinar on MENTAL HEALTH-“Dealing With Uncertainties’’ was held to create awareness and positivity among people during the tough time everyone is going through. Instagram, Facebook and Whatsapp were used as an online platform to inform people about the webinar. We collaborated for the same with the drama club of college –M.O.S.A.I.C and ABHYUDAY ,The Social body of IIT Bombay. Various promotional events were planned for the same. First one was the doodle-art competition ‘DOODLIE-DOO’ in which the participants were asked to draw doodles on the topic- Moods, Mind and Mental Health and were featured on the club’s Instagram page. Around 30 people participated in it and the winners were declared after the webinar. A video, prepared by M.O.S.A.I.C, the drama club of SIT, was shared on Instagram on how mental is being affected during the lockdown. Another event was- any phase in life which they want to talk about and last was the one in which ten simple questions with options ‘YES’ or ‘NO’ were asked on the club’s page. Around 90-100 peoples joined on the day of webinar. The webinar included three panelists who discussed about various issues and how to tackle all the stressful thoughts- Dr. Anuradha Harkare, Founder, Mind Catalyst Study Counselling. She talked about how right now people’s minds are full of various uncertainties and the importance to deal with it. She further discussed how difficult it is for students to adapt to a complete new process of learning, how pattern of exams has changed, and how this all has affected final year students the most. Ms. Anushree Lokhande, Founder, Evolving together. She discussed about how one need to manage emotions and thoughts. Emotions become intense as one try to ignore them, bury them or misidentify them. The topics that were covered were motional awareness, management of emotions, link between thoughts and emotions, mood boosters, etc. Dr. Girija Mahale, Head, Symbiosis Centre of Emotional Well Being. She talked about how people need to convert challenges into opportunities and how one need to think out of the box and try things not done before. She also talked about self care and other things like- physical activity, sleep hygiene, etc. Ms. Mayuri Dutta was the guest speaker and she talked about mindfulness, take things as it comes to us. She further explained how every problem is an opportunity and the need to take care in the present scenario. At the end of the webinar a question and answer session was held where the participants got a chance to interact and discuss their issues with the three exceptional panelists The webinar made everyone understand that even though the life is full of uncertainties, important thing is that one must look for positive aspects and adapt to the changes to lead a stress free life.
