Rotaract Club of Thane North End

Spread a Bite

DATE : October, 2020
EVENT : “International Day of Charity”, food donation drive.
ORGANIZATION: Rotaract Club of Thane North End
LOCATION : Navi Mumbai

On the occasion of International Day of Charity, which is observed annually on 5th of September, the Rotaract Club of Thane North End initiated a food donation drive – SPREAD A BITE from the 4th of September to the 8th of September. The main aim was to provide nutritious food to the underprivileged people. V@rSITy Care has collaborated with the Rotaract Club of Thane North End and we joined this drive in a very responsible and enthusiastic manner. One of our club members , Nikhil Sawant took up the responsibility of donating the food to the underprivileged whereas the other members of V@rSITy Care decided to donate the amount that would be required for the food. The response was astounding. We collected an amount of more than 5000 Rs. Around 50 people were provided food with this amount. V@rSITy Care has decided to continue this drive for another month.
