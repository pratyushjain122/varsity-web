Waste management in Symbiosis Campus

DATE: January 30, 2020

EVENT: Waste management in Symbiosis campus.

ORGANIZATION: Symbiosis Centre for Waste Resource Management

LOCATION: SIT

A guest lecture on “Waste Management in Symbiosis Campus” was held on 30th January 2020. The guest lecture has been organized by the department of Civil Engineering (CESS) and V@rSITy-care to provide the students with an opportunity to learn about the techniques of water management in the campus. The guest lecture began with welcome address by the President of CESS, Ashwini Kumar. He introduced and welcomed Dr. Manikprabhu Dhanorkar, to deliver the lecture Dr. Manikprabhu Dhanorkar, Deputy Head, Symbiosis Centre for Waste Resource Management (SCWRM) has been recognized as a 'Member of Expert Committee' for Solid Waste Management by Mumbai Agricultural Produce Market Committee (MAPMC), Mumbai, a Corporate body, constituted by Govt. of Maharashtra. The session was then continued by Dr Manikprabhu Dhanorkar, who began with an introduction about his research centre and aim of SCWRM following which, he shed light on the ways in which one wastes the resources which are available around us or are in nature. He gave various examples as wastage of food on a daily basis and how can we reduce it. He emphasized on the mentality of people before wasting the resources. He introduced us to the waste management system in the Symbiosis campus(Hill base and Hill top).He further gave the details of the Septic tank, the biogas plant which converts the waste into CNG and usage of bio trucks for recycling plastic bottle. The session ended with a hearty thanks and presenting of a memento to Dr Manikprabhu Dhanorkar by Sachin Jha in appreciation for taking the time out to educate the students on Waste Management. He thanked the Management for providing the platform to organize guest lectures on innovative technologies.
