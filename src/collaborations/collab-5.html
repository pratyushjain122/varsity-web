Karishmaa Care Foundation

Pukaar Donation drive

DATE : 22 August, 2019
EVENT : Pukaar Donation drive for the Maharashtra Flood Victims
ORGANIZATION: Karishmaa Care Foundation
LOCATION: Symbiosis Institute of Technology and near ISCON Temple, Pune Camp

On August 22, 2019 , V@rSITy Care joined hands with Karishmaa Care, an organisation which works for the empowerment of the affected people. All the members worked in shifts to help the victims of ‘Maharashtra Floods’. The team talked to people and convinced them to donate for the victims. A donation drive was also organized in the college which was successful with a collection of INR 33k+. We bought 15kg daal, 15kg poha, 60 bathing soaps, approximately 60 washing bars, 19 floor cleaning liquid bottles, 15 toothpastes, notebooks and school bags worth Rs. 18,600 (approx.). INR 6.5k were donated to the Karishmaa Care foundation. The rest of the money was transferred online to disaster relief fund. The team went room to room and person to person to collect the funds in cash and kind. This small effort from everybody might have put up big, bright smiles on a thousand faces.
