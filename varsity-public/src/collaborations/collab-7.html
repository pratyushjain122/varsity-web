Vasundhara Swachata Abhiyan

Cleanliness Drive

DATE : September 1,2019 â€“ January 26,2020
EVENT : Cleanliness Drive
ORGANIZATION: Vasundhara Swachata Abhiyan
LOCATION: Kirti Garden near Pashan Hill

On September 1st 2019, V@rSITy Care joined hands with an umbrella organization, Vashundhara Swachata Abhiyan, Pune with the mission of reforestation of the hills, afforestation- pure air ,water percolation, waste management and plastic-free lifestyle .

Every Sunday, a team of 5-10 volunteers would leave for the Kirti Garden by 7:00 am bus. Each member was provided with a pair of hand gloves. The team used to head towards the place carrying garbage bags and collected all the plastic as well as the other wastes. Regular cleaning and watering of the area had shown great results as the amount of garbage being collected was reducing every week. The place has been suitable and hygienic for the people and their pets to have morning and evening walks.
