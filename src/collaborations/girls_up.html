International Day of the Girl

DATE: 11th October, 2020

EVENT: International Day of the Girl

VENUE: Virtual

To glorify the International Day of the Girl, a wonderful video was prepared, wherein the female members of the club put forth certain situations, and the male members of the club had to describe how they would react in such situations. The male members gave several thoughtful and considerate opinions and this video which was posted on YouTube and Instagram, turned into a grand success. Additionally, there was an amazing hashtag challenge called #GirlsHurlTheWorld where the female members of the club had to state what they love about being a girl. Also, a story was posted on V@rSITy-Care’s Instagram page, which had a question for it’s viewers: “What if there were no males in the world for 24 hours?”. Various caring and compassionate responses were received and this event too became equally outstanding and marvellous.
